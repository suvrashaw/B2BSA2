## **HOW TO READ THIS DOCUMENT**

Each page entry contains:

* **Target Keywords** — Primary (use X times), Secondary (use Y times), LSI/supporting terms  
* **Meta Title & Meta Description** — Ready to deploy  
* **H1, H2, H3 hierarchy** — Full content skeleton  
* **CTA Placements** — Where to place calls-to-action  
* **Content Notes** — Tone, intent, UX guidance

**Keyword Density Rule (Global):**

* Primary keyword: **1.0–1.5%** density (roughly 1 use per 100 words)  
* Secondary keywords: **0.5–0.8%** density  
* LSI/supporting: Sprinkle naturally, no hard target  
* **Never stuff.** Google penalizes over-optimization above 2.5%
